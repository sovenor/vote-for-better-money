VOTE FOR BETTER MONEY

Our mission is to show Americans they can vote for better money.

You can help by putting up a flyer in public.

Each flyer has a QR code that links to an educational page about Bitcoin.

Print these flyers out and put them in places people will see them.

Most coffee shops (and other businesses) have public bulletin boards where you can put up a flyer.

You can also put these outside on telephone poles using tape. Get creative!
