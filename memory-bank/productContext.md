# Product Context — Vote For Better Money

## Why This Project Exists
Americans are frustrated by rising prices and inflation, but most don't understand the root cause: unlimited money printing and growing national debt. This site exists to bridge that knowledge gap and present Bitcoin as a practical, actionable alternative — not just an investment, but a better form of money.

## Problems It Solves
1. **Knowledge gap:** Most Americans don't understand why prices keep rising or what causes inflation
2. **Learned helplessness:** People feel they can only influence the economy through voting, which rarely delivers results
3. **Bitcoin intimidation:** Many people have heard of Bitcoin but find it confusing or risky
4. **Misinformation:** Bitcoin is often misrepresented; this site corrects common misconceptions with data

## How It Works
- **Homepage** makes the case: shows real-time data comparing Bitcoin's performance vs. dollar depreciation, visualizes money supply growth and national debt, then presents Bitcoin's properties (scarce, decentralized, permissionless, sovereign, American)
- **Learn section** provides 5 deep-dive articles on Bitcoin's key properties
- **How to Use Bitcoin** gives practical step-by-step guidance (buy, earn, self-custody)
- **Flyers** enables grassroots distribution with a downloadable PDF
- **Links** provides a shareable page for social media outreach
- **Real-time stats** from FRED, TwelveData, and Mempool.space keep the data current and credible

## User Experience Goals
- **Accessible tone:** Written for everyday Americans, not crypto insiders
- **Politically neutral:** Criticizes both parties equally for debt/inflation
- **Data-driven:** Real-time stats make the argument tangible and credible
- **Mobile-first:** Clean, dark-themed design that works great on phones
- **Action-oriented:** Every page leads toward actually using Bitcoin
- **Fast:** ISR caching, minimal dependencies, optimized images
